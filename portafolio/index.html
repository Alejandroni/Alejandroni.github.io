SKILLS

CINEMAGRAPH

Cinemagraphs are photographs in which a minor and repeated motion occurs. Thanks to this, you can get more of the photographic compositions, creating new ideas, highlighting details from another perspective, creating a unique visual piece or taking much
more value to veryday photograph.
